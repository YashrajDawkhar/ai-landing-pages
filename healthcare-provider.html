About Dr. Sarah Johnson

15+ Years
Experience

Board Certified Family Physician

Dr. Sarah Johnson brings over 15 years of medical experience to her practice. After graduating with honors from Harvard Medical School, she completed her residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital where she was recognized for her exceptional patient care.

Her approach combines evidence-based medicine with compassionate care, ensuring that each patient receives personalized treatment plans designed for their unique needs. Dr. Johnson specializes in family medicine with additional expertise in preventive care and women's health.
